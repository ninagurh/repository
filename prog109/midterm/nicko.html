Nicko the pug

He is my 15-year old dog and taking care of him is something I absolutely love doing. He knows a few tricks like sit, spin, and jump, but has never learned to play fetch. Still, he's the one of the most gentle and silliest canine I've ever met, and I am not just saying that because he's my dog.

Facts

Here are three fun facts about him:

  1. He is cock-eyed. You can never take the guy seriously.
  2. His tongue is always sticking out, giving us a silly smile.
  3. Like most pugs, he tilts his head as if he's confused when you ask him a question.
